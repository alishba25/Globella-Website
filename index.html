A Global Summer Celebration

Globella – The International Fest

Globella is a two-day summer festival in Winnipeg that brings together cultures from around the world through food, music, performances, and local businesses.

It creates a vibrant space where communities share their traditions while visitors discover global experiences all in one place.

Designed as a lively outdoor celebration, Globella highlights the diversity that makes the city unique while creating a fun and welcoming atmosphere for everyone.
